## Placement

- LXC `109` `docmost` on `pve1`.

## Status

- Docmost is still deployed and reachable but **not** the canonical documentation surface.
- Fumadocs (this site) is the canonical documentation layer.

## What to do with it

The decision is binary: migrate any surviving Docmost content into Fumadocs, then either keep Docmost online as a read-only archive or decommission the LXC entirely. This is tracked on the [migration plan](/architecture/migration-plan).

## Dependencies

- Docmost typically uses PostgreSQL. The PostgreSQL LXC (`112`) on `pve1` is the most likely backend, but the exact wiring is not exported into this documentation.

## Failure impact

- During the transition window, a Docmost outage means previously-written content is unreachable until either Docmost is restored or that content has been mirrored into Fumadocs.
