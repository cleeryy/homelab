---
title: Pre-production
description: Staging environment for services before they reach production.
---

## Purpose

Validate configuration changes, upgrades, and new services before they touch production workloads.

## Target state

- **Host node:** `pve3`, in a clearly isolated namespace
- **Role:** Lab
- **Storage:** Application data tier with shorter retention; no bulk allocation
- **Ingress:** Internal only

## Conventions

- Pre-prod workloads are named with a `pp-` prefix.
- Pre-prod never shares persistent volumes with production.
- Pre-prod is excluded from production backups.

## Notes

If a pre-prod workload needs to ingest production data, do it through a one-off export, not a live link.
