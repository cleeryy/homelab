### `pve3`

- Bridges:
  - `vmbr0` — management LAN.
  - `vmbr1` — routed internal network, host IP `10.0.0.124`.
  - `vmbr2` — currently down, holds `192.168.1.112` (purpose pending validation).
- Storage attached:
  - `local` — host directory.
  - `local-lvm` — large LVM-thin pool, ~2.6 TB.
  - `nas` — NFS share, shared with `pve1`.
  - Additional physical disks passed through to the TrueNAS VM (`127`).
- No active ZFS pool on the host.
- CPU, RAM, and disk slot map: **pending capture**.

### `pve1`

- Bridges:
  - `vmbr0` — management LAN.
- Storage attached:
  - `local` — host directory.
  - `local-lvm` — LVM-thin.
  - `nas` — NFS share, shared with `pve3`.
  - `pve1-storage` — ZFS pool, ~1.09 TB usable.
- CPU, RAM, and disk slot map: **pending capture**.
