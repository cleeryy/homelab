This page captures **transitions that are already visible** in the live setup, not aspirational moves. It is short by design.

## Documentation: Docmost → Fumadocs

- Docmost runs as LXC `109` on `pve1`.
- This Fumadocs site is the canonical documentation surface going forward.
- Action: migrate any surviving Docmost content into Fumadocs, then decide whether to keep Docmost online for archive access or decommission the LXC.

## Cluster: `pve2`

- `pve2` is not active but `pve2-storage` is still defined in `/etc/pve/storage.cfg`.
- Action: confirm whether `pve2` should be formally removed from the cluster and whether the `pve2-storage` entry should be cleaned up.

## Ingress: two reverse-proxy VMs

- `ubuntu-nginx-proxy-manager` (VM `100`) and `ubuntu-nginxrpm` (VM `105`) both live on `pve1`.
- The split between them is not confirmed.
- Action: decide which is the canonical ingress and either decommission or repurpose the other.

## Network: `vmbr2`

- `vmbr2` on `pve3` holds `192.168.1.112` but is currently **down**.
- Action: decide whether this bridge is required, repurpose it, or remove it from the configuration.
