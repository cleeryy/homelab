## Purpose

Authoritative mapping of services to nodes, with the rationale. Placement should never be inferred from the running system.

## Target placement

Services are grouped by role. Apps and media live on `pve3`; platform, ingress, and observability live on `pve1`.

## Placement rules

- A service must not move nodes without updating this table.
- Lab and pre-production workloads stay on `pve3` and never on `pve1`.
- `pve2` does not host services.

## Notes

Items marked **Migrate** are tracked in the [migration plan](/architecture/migration-plan). Items marked **Decommission** are tracked in [Operations > Decommissioning](/operations/decommissioning).
