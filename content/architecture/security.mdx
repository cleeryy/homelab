## Purpose

Define where trust boundaries are drawn and how access to the homelab is controlled.

## Scope

Design intent and policy. Concrete credentials, keys, and per-service hardening live with each service page or out-of-band, never here.

## Target state

- **Trust boundaries**
  - Public internet → perimeter firewall → reverse proxy → service.
  - Management plane is not reachable from user-facing networks.
  - Lab workloads are isolated from production segments.
- **Identity & access**
  - SSH access is key-based; passwords are disabled where supported.
  - Web services sit behind the reverse proxy with TLS terminated centrally.
  - Administrative access is restricted to the management segment.
- **Secrets**
  - Stored out-of-band, never committed to the documentation repo.
  - Rotation policy is documented per service.
- **Patching**
  - Cadence and method are defined in [Operations > Patching](/operations/patching).

## Notes

Do not document specific credentials, ports, or hostnames in this page. Reference services and operations pages instead.
