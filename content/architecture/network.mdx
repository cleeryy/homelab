## Purpose

Describe how the network is segmented, how traffic flows between segments, and where the trust boundaries sit.

## Scope

Logical design only. Cable diagrams and physical port maps belong under [Inventory > Networks](/inventory/networks).

## Target state

- VLAN-segmented network with clear roles per segment (management, services, lab, storage, guest).
- Inter-VLAN routing handled by the perimeter firewall; default-deny between segments unless explicitly allowed.
- Management plane is reachable only from a trusted segment.
- Ingress to user-facing services goes through a single reverse proxy.
