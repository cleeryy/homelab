## Purpose

Summarise the target homelab as a single coherent system: which nodes exist, what each one is responsible for, and how workloads flow between them.

## Scope

This page is a map, not a manual. It points to the detailed pages in this section rather than restating their content.

## Target state

- Two Proxmox VE nodes form the platform:
  - `pve3` — primary compute, runs production VMs and containers.
  - `pve1` — infrastructure and management plane (identity, monitoring, backups, ingress).
- Storage, network, and backups are designed independently of the workloads so services can be relocated between nodes without redesign.
- Every service has a defined home node and a documented failover or recovery posture.
