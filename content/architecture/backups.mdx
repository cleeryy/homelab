## Purpose

Describe how the homelab is backed up, what the recovery objectives are, and where backups physically live.

## Scope

Policy and design only. The day-to-day procedure is in [Operations > Backup & restore](/operations/backup-restore).

## Target state

- All stateful services have a documented backup path before they are considered in production.
- Backups land in a **separate failure domain** from the primary data — different node, different pool, ideally different power source.
- Restore is rehearsed on a defined cadence; an untested backup is not a backup.
