## Purpose

Provide a predictable response procedure when something breaks, so triage is fast and follow-up is consistent.

## Triage

- [ ] Identify the affected service and its scope (single service, segment, node, whole platform).
- [ ] Check [Architecture > Service placement](/architecture/service-placement) to confirm impact.
- [ ] Look at monitoring dashboards for correlated signals.

## Mitigate

- [ ] Apply the smallest change that restores service (restart, rollback, failover).
- [ ] Avoid configuration drift: any emergency change is followed by a documented update.

## Recover

- [ ] If a backup restore is needed, follow [Backup & restore](/operations/backup-restore).
- [ ] Validate the service end-to-end before declaring resolution.

## Follow-up

- [ ] Write a short postmortem: what happened, what was done, what changes are needed.
- [ ] Update affected documentation (architecture, service page, runbook) so the next incident is shorter.
